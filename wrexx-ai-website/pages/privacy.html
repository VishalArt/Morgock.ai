Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

MorGock is an agentic AI platform for enterprises. We are based in Gurugram, India. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@MorGock.

2. What Data We Collect

When you create an account or fill out a form on our site, we may collect your name, email address, company name, and any messages you send us. We do not collect payment card details directly.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to respond to enquiries, provide our services, send product updates (with your consent), and improve our platform. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Storage

Account credentials entered on the sign-in page are stored locally in your own browser (localStorage) and are never transmitted to our servers. Form submissions are processed via Google Apps Script and stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to MorGock administrators.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies and browser storage only to maintain your session. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at privacy@MorGock. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
